About the Supplier ADP Pilot

The CVE Program Supplier ADP (Authorized Data Publisher) Pilot allows CNAs that are also product vendors/suppliers to add enrichment data to CVE records for CVEs assigned by other CNAs that affect their products.

What is enrichment?

Suppliers add data such as affected product versions, CVSS scores, references, and descriptions to CVE records where they have product-specific knowledge.

How are suppliers identified?

SADP containers have x_adpType: "supplier" or a shortName ending in -SADP in the containers.adp[] array.

Data source

Records are sourced from the Published SADP Records directory in the CVEProject/sadp-pilot repository.
